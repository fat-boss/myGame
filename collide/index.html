Each puzzle consists of a set of balls placed on the board. To solve a puzzle, you need to remove all balls(but one) from the board

You do this by "flinging" balls along a line into other balls, cansing the hit balls to roll off the board

You cannot fling a ball into a neighbouring ball

Control: use your mouse select a ball

W/↑ - Move selector up

S/↓ - Move selector down

A/← - Move selector left

D/→ - Move selector right

Tips: Use the Undo/R when you get stuck
